Daily wellness tips

🌄
Dinacharya — Morning Routine
Wake before sunrise, scrape your tongue, drink warm water with lemon, and practice oil pulling with sesame oil for oral health.
🌿
Seasonal Eating
Eat according to your dosha and the season. Favour warm, cooked foods in winter; cool, light foods in summer.
🧖
Abhyanga — Self-Massage
Daily warm oil massage calms Vata, nourishes the skin, improves circulation, and promotes deep, restful sleep.
